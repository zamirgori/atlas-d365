About this application

Atlas is a web-based knowledge and learning tool for Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations functional consultants. It transforms the Microsoft Business Process Catalog into a navigable, hierarchical, end-to-end view of business processes — with drill-down flow diagrams, D365 product coverage mapping, APQC industry framework alignment, and full system-level traceability.

Data source — Microsoft Business Process Catalog

All process data is sourced exclusively from the Microsoft Business Process Catalog (BPC) — Microsoft's official taxonomy of business processes and how Dynamics 365 applications support them. The catalog covers the full hierarchy: End-to-End Process → Process Area → Process → Scenario → System Process → Test Case.

Official documentation: Microsoft Business Process Catalog — learn.microsoft.com

How to use Atlas

Navigate the tree on the left to explore all 15 end-to-end process families. Click any node to open its content. The Process Flow tab (first tab) shows a drill-down visual diagram of all child nodes at that level. Use the Application Family and Product filters to reshape the tree to your project scope. Press ⌘K (Mac) or Ctrl+K (Windows) to search across all 5,873 nodes.
